Land Registry Digital Service

The Land Registry are working with The Government Digital Service (GDS) to build a new digital service. The service will form a part of GOV.UK and is currently in alpha phase following guidance in the Government Service Design Manual in order to meet the Government Digital By Default Service Standard.

The service is being built in the open, with the code developed as open source, and the architecture and other documentation published under the open government licence.

Digital Land Charges

P334 Digital Land Charges work package - The following functions are carried out by the Land Charges department:

• Agricultural Credits
• Bankruptcy
• Land Charges

The current IT solution for Land Charges is difficult to maintain, being developed many years ago and using old technology. There is currently a reliance upon paper based systems and processes (particularly in the Agricultural Credits arena). With the new Insolvency rules that are expected to come into effect April 2016, there is an opportunity to work with the Insolvency Service to provide a better user experience to benefit all.

The current Alpha activities are to create a digital service to enable Business to Business (B2B) automated processing between Insolvency Service and the Land Charges Bankruptcy Index.

Subject to passing the GDS Service Delivery Assessment at end of Alpha, the service will move into a wider scoped Beta phase.
