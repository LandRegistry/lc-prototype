Apply for Alcohol Wholesaler Registration Scheme (AWRS)

The Alcohol Wholesaler Registration Scheme (AWRS) applies to existing, and new, wholesalers of alcohol, trading at or after the point at which excise duty has been paid. All businesses who sell alcohol will need to make sure that any of their UK wholesalers are registered with HMRC. Make sure you have read the information relating to AWRS before completing the application.

Once you start the application, you have 28 days to complete it. You can save your progress at any time.

Before you start

To complete the application you may need:

  • the addresses of any additional premises
  • the full names, dates of birth, National Insurance numbers or Passport numbers of all business Directors and Company Secretaries connected to alcohol wholesale
  • the names, addresses and VAT numbers of up to five of your main alcohol suppliers by units received

You will need to sign in with your Government Gateway account. If you don't have a Government Gateway account, you can sign up on the next page.
