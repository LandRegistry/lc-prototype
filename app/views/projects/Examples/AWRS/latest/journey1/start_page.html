Apply for Alcohol Wholesaler Registration Scheme (AWRS)

The Alcohol Wholesaler Registration Scheme (AWRS) applies to existing, and new, wholesalers of alcohol, trading at or after the point at which excise duty has been paid. Make sure you have read the information relating to AWRS before completing the application. Once you start the application, you have 28 days to complete it before it is deleted.

Use this service if you are an existing or new alcohol wholesaler.

Before you start

To complete the application you may need:

  • a UTR number
  • the addresses of your additional trading premises
  • the full names, for UK residents National Insurance numbers, for non-UK residents Passport numbers or the Identity card numbers of all business directors and company secretaries or partners
  • the names, addresses and VAT numbers of your five largest alcohol suppliers by value

You will need to sign in with your Government Gateway account. If you don't have a Government Gateway account, you can sign up on the next page.
