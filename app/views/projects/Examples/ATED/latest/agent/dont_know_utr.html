Unique Taxpayer Reference (UTR)

This is only issued by HM Revenue & Customs (HMRC) when you or your client, have registered as business.

It is made up of 10 numbers, for example 12345 67890, and can be found on documentation issued by HMRC. It may be printed next to the headings 'Tax Reference', 'UTR' or 'Official Use'.

Where to find this

You can find your UTR reference in the header of any of the letter that you received from HMRC.
